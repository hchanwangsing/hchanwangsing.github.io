RoboCare: Humanoid Robotics Tactile Upper Limb

  • By Chan Wang Sing, Shilong Mu, Runze Zhao, Xinyue Chai
  • 2024
  • Tsinghua University

The global caregiver shortage is intensifying. Exisiting humanoid robots, often lacking advanced tactile sensing, fall short in performing delicate tasks essential for caregiving. RoboCare addresses this by introducing a tactile robotic upper limb for humanoid robots. Featuring a rigid-soft hybrid arm and a dexterous hand. A unified control strategy integrates these components, enabling robots to perform fine manipulations.
