High Performance Multiaxis Triboelectric Nanogenerator for Blue Energy Harvesting

  • ByChan Wang Sing
  • 2023
  • Tsinghua University

This project develops a high-performance, omnidirectional triboelectric nanogenerator (TENG) for harvesting blue energy from ocean waves. The spherical TENG integrates multilayered dielectric interfaces and liquid-solid TENGs (LS-TENGs) to capture mechanical energy from water wave motion in all directions. The device achieves a peak open-circuit voltage of 100V under single-axis oscillations at 1.8Hz and demonstrates consistent multi-axial energy harvesting performance. Numerical simulations validate the electric potential distribution, while practical tests confirm the systemâ€™s reliability in tranquil and dynamic sea conditions. This innovative approach enhances the feasibility of sustainable ocean energy harvesting to meet global renewable energy goals.
